Yes. The platform is completely free to use. We earn a commission from the broker when you trade, so there is no cost to you.

Three independent ML models (LightGBM, XGBoost, CatBoost) analyze 100+ features. A trade is only placed when all three models agree on direction, achieving a 75% win rate in backtesting.
What is the minimum deposit?
Exness has no fixed minimum for most account types. We recommend starting with at least $100 and using 0.01 lot size to manage risk properly.
